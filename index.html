About

My name is Ryan Doherty. I hail from Kansas City, and have worked in Event and Live Entertainment Production for five years. This includes time spent with top-tier groups and events including The PGA Tour, The Kansas City Chiefs, The Web.com Tour, AEG Live, and many others.

More recently however, I have transitioned into a field I have always been fascinated by — Web Development. This began as a journey to build my own personal Freelance brand and combat many of the inefficiencies I see in the day-to-day production of Events, but quickly developed into a passion for elegantly solving complicated problems across all fields by way of code. My journey began with a self-taught approach, and has steadily continued thanks to countless Meetups, and the LaunchCode educational program.
